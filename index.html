Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 26, 2026

Welcome to Better ("the App"), operated by Brody ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use the App:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile details provided during registration or through a connected coaching platform (e.g., TrueCoach).
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, session duration, and interaction events within the App.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Photos: Only when you voluntarily attach a screenshot while submitting a support or issue report. Photos are never accessed without your action.
  • Audio: The App may play audio content (e.g., podcasts, lessons) in the background. We do not record or store your audio.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's features and content.
  • Personalize your experience (e.g., courses, nutrition plans, coaching sessions).
  • Respond to support requests and issue reports.
  • Send important App updates or notifications (you may opt out at any time).
  • Analyze usage trends to enhance performance and user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with third-party services that have their own privacy policies. These include:

  • TrueCoach – for coaching and training plan delivery. We open TrueCoach content within the App or redirect to the TrueCoach platform. See TrueCoach's Privacy Policy.
  • Typeform – for forms and check-ins (e.g., Weekly Check-In, Rapid Fire). See Typeform's Privacy Policy.
  • Expo / EAS – for app delivery and over-the-air updates. See Expo's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is processed and stored using industry-standard security measures. While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and keep your device software up to date.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out of certain data uses or communications.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email below.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
